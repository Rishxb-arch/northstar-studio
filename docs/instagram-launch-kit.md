# Northstar Studio — Instagram Launch Kit

## Positioning

**One-line:** Your AI management agency for Instagram/TikTok creators.

**Short bio:** We study trends, competitors, audience signals, and brand deals — then turn them into Reels/TikToks creators can actually film.

**Page promise:** Stop guessing what to post next. Build a creator brain, get weekly video briefs, and run your creator business like a media company.

1. `@northstarstudio` — cleanest if available

Recommended first choice: **Northstar Studio** / `@northstarstudio` if available.

## Visual direction

- Dark premium background: black/charcoal
- Accent: acid green/yellow
- Typography: bold editorial headlines + small mono labels
- Motif: “creator brain”, dashboards, agent cards, signal/radar visuals
- Avoid generic robot stock art. Make it feel like a modern creator operating system.

## Content pillars

1. **Creator pain** — why creators need a team, not another caption tool.
2. **Agent swarm** — brand brain, trend scout, competitor radar, reel planner, deal agent.
3. **Reel strategy** — hooks, formats, scripts, retention, shot lists.
4. **Creator economy thesis** — every creator becomes a business.
5. **Build in public** — product screens, experiments, case studies, beta calls.
